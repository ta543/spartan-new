Instead of going flat overtime, Nuzzle retains the needed bulk and support for over 1001 nights.

They're designed to balance the softness and support you need when you sleep, continually adapting to the weight of your head and sleep position.

2. Experience Perfect Spinal Alignment In Any Sleep Position

No single pillow works best for everyone. That's why Nuzzle's outer shell houses 2 inner layers that can be used separately or together to create your dream pillow.

You can customize the amount of neck support for better spine health and fewer visits to the chiropractor.

Stream

The soft layer provides support and added softness and plushiness. It's perfect for stomach sleepers to keep your necks totally levelled.

The medium layer is designed to maintain straight spinal alignment from the neck down, and is perfect for back or combination sleepers.

Using both layers provides ultimate firmness and is perfect for keeping neck and spinal alignment for side sleepers.

The outer shell of the Nuzzle pillow reacts to your body temperature, absorbing heat from you when you're hot, and releasing it back to you as you cool down - keeping you at the optimal temperature of 37.5°C.
